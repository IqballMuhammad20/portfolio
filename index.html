About Me

I am a UI/UX Designer with over 4 years of experience in creating user-centered digital design solutions. My core competencies include building scalable design systems, applying mobile-first design strategies, and ensuring accessibility across all digital interfaces. Throughout my career, I have worked closely with cross-functional teams to deliver design solutions that are not only visually appealing but also functional, inclusive, and aligned with business goals. I am proficient in tools such as Figma, Sketch, and Adobe XD, and I apply user research and usability testing as key components in my design process. I believe effective design solves real problems, prioritizes user needs, and contributes meaningfully to product success and business growth.
